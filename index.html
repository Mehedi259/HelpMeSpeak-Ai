Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the translations?

HelpMeSpeak uses advanced AI and Google Cloud Translation API to provide highly accurate translations. For best results, use clear and simple sentences. Context-dependent phrases may vary, but we continuously improve our algorithms.

Can I use HelpMeSpeak offline?

The Phrasebook feature works offline once you've saved your favorite phrases. However, Instant Translation and AI Chat require an internet connection for real-time processing.

How does the AI Chat feature work?

Our AI Chat uses advanced language models to understand your message in any language, respond naturally, and help you practice conversations. It can correct grammar, suggest better phrases, and adapt to your learning pace.

Is my translation history saved?

Yes! Your translation history is automatically saved in your account. You can view, search, and delete past translations from your profile settings at any time.

How do I add phrases to my personal phrasebook?

When viewing any phrase or translation, tap the bookmark icon to save it to your personal collection. Access your saved phrases from the Phrasebook section anytime.

Can I translate images with text?

Yes! Use our camera feature to take a photo of signs, menus, documents, or any text, and HelpMeSpeak will detect and translate the text instantly.

Are there any usage limits?

Free users get unlimited basic translations and access to the phrasebook. AI Chat has a daily limit for free users. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited AI conversations and advanced features.

Technical Support

The app is not translating correctly

Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Try refreshing the app or switching to a different network. If using voice, check your microphone permissions in device settings.

Voice input is not working

Check that microphone permissions are enabled for HelpMeSpeak in your device settings. Ensure you're in a quiet environment for better voice recognition.

Camera translation feature not working

Enable camera permissions in your device settings. Ensure adequate lighting and hold your device steady for clear text recognition.

AI Chat responses are slow

This may be due to high server load or slow internet. AI processing takes a few seconds. Check your connection speed and try again.

My saved phrases disappeared

Ensure you're logged into the correct account. Saved phrases are synced with your account. If the issue persists, contact support with your account details.
