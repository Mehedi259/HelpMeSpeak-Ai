Frequently Asked Questions

Does HelpMeSpeak use AI?

Yes. HelpMeSpeak uses trusted third-party AI and translation services to generate translations and AI chat responses.

Is internet required?

AI Chat and live translation require an internet connection. Saved phrases can be accessed offline.

Privacy & Data Security

Your privacy matters to us. We are transparent about what data we collect, how it is used, and when it is shared.

  • User-provided content such as text, voice, or images is collected only when features are used
  • AI Chat and translation features may send user input to third-party AI service providers
  • Third-party services are used strictly for app functionality
  • User consent is required before any data is sent to AI services
  • No personal data is sold or used for advertising
  • Users can delete their data from within the app
  • All data is encrypted during transmission
  • User data is securely stored using Firebase infrastructure

AI Services & User Consent

What data is shared with AI services?

Yes. Before using AI-powered features, HelpMeSpeak asks for your explicit consent. You may decline or disable AI features at any time.
